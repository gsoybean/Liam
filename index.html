Description
This mystical bespoke wall mural has the perfect blend of chalky whites and deep, rich blues creating a gorgeous midnight garden setting. Calming and elegant this beautiful venetian floral design has been taken from the Graham & Brown archive, the stunning trailing branches are perfectly finished with a perched bird to give a sophisticated, opulent look.
